## Our Story

The **Seattle Baseball Academy** was launched in 2009 as **City Baseball**. From the beginning, we've been known as a welcoming, supportive community that cares about players both on and off the field.

What started as a small group of dedicated coaches and players has grown into the Pacific Northwest's premier youth baseball organization.

## Our Mission

We develop baseball players through:

- **Great Coaching**: Our staff includes coaches with professional playing experience
- **Elite Competition**: We provide opportunities to compete at the highest levels
- **Year-Round Training**: Our 14,000-square-foot indoor facility means no off-season

## Our Philosophy

We believe in developing the whole player. Baseball skills are important, but so are:

- **Character**: We build young men of integrity
- **Work Ethic**: Success comes from consistent effort
- **Team First**: Individual achievement serves the team
- **Love of the Game**: Players who enjoy baseball improve faster

## Our Community

Seattle Baseball Academy is more than a baseball program—it's a community. Our families support each other, our alumni come back to help, and our coaches are invested in each player's success.
