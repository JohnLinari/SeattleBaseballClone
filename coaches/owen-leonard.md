        <h1>Owen Leonard</h1>
        <p class="title">Strength and Conditioning Director, 18U Head Coach</p>
        <div class="bio">
            <p>Owen began as the Strength and Conditioning and Head Coach in 2022 following the close of his four-year career on the pitching staff at Washington State University.</p>
            <p>Owen is a City baseball alumni and Ballard High School graduate. He began his college career at Pacific University, pitched one season at Everett CC before transferring to WSU.</p>
            <p>At WSU, Owen was recognized by the Athletic Department for his leadership qualities and appointed to serve on the school's Student Athlete Advisory Committee. He graduated with a degree in Kinesiology, and applies an advanced set of skills to his new role.</p>
            <p>He is coaching the 18U Showcase team with <a href="{{ '/coaches/wes-long/' | relative_url }}">Wes Long</a>.</p>
