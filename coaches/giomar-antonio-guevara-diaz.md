            <p>Coach Guevara also played professional winter ball from 1993 to 2001 with the Leones del Caracas and Tigres de Aragua clubs of the Venezuelan League.</p>
            <p>Giomar began coaching with us in 2021. He has taken a prominent role coaching players at all age levels in our off-season program. He has taken on 18U head coaching duties while contributing to on field management at 13U and 14U.</p>
