        <h1>Todd Linden</h1>
        <p class="title">Off-Season Program Lead and Underclass Head Coach</p>
        <div class="bio">
            <p>Todd is responsible for running the off-season program for our most competitive teams. He has also served as the field manager for our most competitive teams aged 13 to 18, and works directly with players who are planning to play college baseball. Todd was a three-sport athlete at Central Kitsap High School where he graduated in 1998. He went on to play baseball at the University of Washington (1999-2000). After an exceptional Cape Cod league season (.535 slugging), he transferred to Louisiana State University where he hit for a .312 AVG, .609 SLG, with 20 HRs and 14 2Bs. Drafted in the 2001 MLB draft as the 41st overall pick by the San Francisco Giants, Todd played 11 years of professional baseball, including five MLB seasons, and two seasons in Japan. Todd and his wife Rachel reside in Edmonds, WA. They have a daughter (Riley) and son (Troy).</p>
