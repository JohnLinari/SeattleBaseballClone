Seattle Baseball Academy is a premier baseball training facility located in Ballard, Washington, dedicated to the development of young athletes. The academy features multiple indoor hitting cages, dedicated pitching lanes, and a full-service weight room designed for strength and athletic performance. Utilizing cutting-edge technology including TrackMan, Rapsodo, HitTrax, and Blast Motion, athletes receive advanced, data-driven instruction to enhance pitching, hitting, and overall player development. Built for year-round training, Seattle Baseball Academy provides a comprehensive environment where players can train, analyze, and elevate their game.

## Facility & Cage Rental

Seattle Baseball Academy offers facility and cage rentals for teams, coaches, and individuals looking for a dedicated training space. Our state-of-the-art facility features multiple hitting cages, pitching lanes, and a full-service weight room, providing everything needed for skill development and practice. Rentals are available by the hour, giving players and teams the flexibility to train on their schedule in a professional, fully equipped environment.
