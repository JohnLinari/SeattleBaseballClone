## Technology

We use modern technology to enhance player development:

- **Video Analysis**: Break down mechanics frame by frame
- **Pitch Tracking**: Measure velocity, spin rate, and movement
- **Exit Velocity**: Track hitting power development
- **Data-Driven Coaching**: Use metrics to guide training

## Location

Our facility is conveniently located in Seattle, easily accessible from throughout the Puget Sound region.

The facility is available for team rentals and private events. Contact us for availability and rates.
