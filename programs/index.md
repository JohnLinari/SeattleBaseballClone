<section class="content">

Our instructors help players improve game-specific skills, strength, and conditioning. With exclusive use of our 14,000-square-foot indoor training facility, we play baseball in Seattle year round.

## Private Instruction

One-on-one training sessions with our experienced coaches. Work on hitting, pitching, fielding, or any specific area you want to improve.

[Learn more about Private Instruction →]({{ '/programs/private-instruction/' | relative_url }})

## Camps & Clinics

Seasonal camps during school breaks and focused clinics throughout the year. Great for intensive skill development in a short period.

[Learn more about Camps & Clinics →]({{ '/programs/camps-clinics/' | relative_url }})

## Cubs & Bears

Our introduction programs for the youngest players. Cubs (ages 5-7) and Bears (ages 8-9) learn baseball fundamentals in a fun, supportive environment.

[Learn more about Cubs & Bears →]({{ '/programs/cubs-bears/' | relative_url }})

## Sim League

Competitive simulation league that provides year-round game experience. Players get meaningful at-bats and game situations even in the off-season.

[Learn more about Sim League →]({{ '/programs/sim-league/' | relative_url }})

## Hitting Group

The Todd Linden Hitting Group offers specialized hitting instruction from former MLB player Todd Linden.
