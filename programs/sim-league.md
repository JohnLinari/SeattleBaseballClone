## What is Sim League?

Sim League provides competitive game situations year-round. Using our indoor facility, players get meaningful at-bats, pitching appearances, and defensive reps even during the off-season.

## How It Works

- **Draft Format**: Players are drafted onto teams for balanced competition
- **Weekly Games**: Regular game schedule throughout the session
- **Stats Tracked**: Full statistics kept for all players
- **Playoffs**: Season culminates in playoff tournament

## Benefits

- Stay sharp during the off-season
- More at-bats than traditional practice
- Game-speed reps in realistic situations
- Competition against quality players
- Fun, engaging format

## Age Divisions

- **Youth Division**: Ages 10-12
- **Teen Division**: Ages 13-15
- **High School Division**: Ages 16-18

## Sessions

Sim League runs multiple sessions throughout the year, typically during fall and winter when outdoor play is limited.
