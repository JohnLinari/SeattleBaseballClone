## Seasonal Camps

Our camps run during school breaks—winter, spring, and summer—providing intensive training opportunities when players have time to focus on development.

### What Camps Include

- Full-day programming
- Skill instruction in all areas of the game
- Scrimmages and game situations
- Strength and conditioning
- Lunch and snacks provided

## Focused Clinics

Throughout the year, we offer specialized clinics focusing on specific skills:

- **Hitting Clinics**: Mechanics, approach, and live at-bats
- **Pitching Clinics**: Arm care, mechanics, pitch development
- **Catching Clinics**: Receiving, blocking, throwing, game management
- **Fielding Clinics**: Position-specific training for infield and outfield

## Age Groups

- **Youth Camps**: Ages 8-12
- **Advanced Camps**: Ages 13-18
- **Position-Specific Clinics**: Varies by clinic

## Registration

Camp and clinic registration opens several weeks before each session. Contact us to be added to our notification list.
