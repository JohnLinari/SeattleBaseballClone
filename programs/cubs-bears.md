## Cubs Program (Ages 5-7)

The Cubs program introduces the youngest players to baseball in a fun, low-pressure environment. Players learn basic skills through games and activities designed to build coordination, confidence, and a love for the game.

### Cubs Focus Areas

- Throwing and catching basics
- Batting fundamentals
- Base running
- Teamwork and sportsmanship
- Fun!

## Bears Program (Ages 8-9)

The Bears program builds on Cubs fundamentals with more structured instruction. Players begin to develop baseball-specific skills while still emphasizing fun and positive experiences.

### Bears Focus Areas

- Refined throwing mechanics
- Fielding ground balls and fly balls
- Hitting approach and mechanics
- Position introduction
- Game situations

## Program Structure

- Weekly sessions during the school year
- Small coach-to-player ratios
- Indoor facility means no weather cancellations
- End-of-season celebration
